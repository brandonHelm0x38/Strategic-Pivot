Goal Definition - Work-Orders, Inventory License-Plate Nesting/Tracking, Master Carton & Unit Nesting/Coding:
* Work-Orders are for accounting purposes and process automation.
* Work-Orders can be comprised of multiple License-Plates. Though this may be displayed in differing styles/formats.

* License-Plates are for inventory tracking and process automation.
* License-Plates can be comprised of Work-Orders & other License-Plates. Though this may be displayed in differing styles/formats.

* Active-Plates universally nest, but have designations...
Inventory Transfers - PLT, BOX, CNT / SHP, PLN, TRN, TRK
EndPoint Sale - PCL->To Consumer, RCT->Receipt
* Multi-Packs aka Nested Carton's contain other Nested Cartons... or Base Cartons... which contain individual units.

In accounting... it's useful to pair EIN's with Divisions of a company. In tracking... you want Facility details.
